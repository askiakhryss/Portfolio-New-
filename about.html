Who am I?

That's a question I ask myself every day—usually while staring at the fridge. Throughout this website, I hope to give you a lens into my world and who I am. It's nice to see you here!

I am Askia Khryss

Curious mind, exploring the tech-human connection

Have you ever thought about what happens when you blend a tech enthusiast, an artist, and a budding scientist? You get me, Askia. I'm on this wild ride of curiosity, always pursuing the next "what if?" and "why not?"

I've immersed myself in the world of tech through programs like Sci-Mi and TKS. These experiences ignited a passion for uncovering a unique intersection of AI, biology, and physics. It’s like discovering a hidden level in a favorite video game—unexpected, thrilling, and brimming with possibilities. However, my creative side refuses to be sidelined. My journey really began with sketching cartoon characters as a child, and now I bring movie stars to life with charcoal. It's been an evolution from stick figures to 3D renderings. Recently, I've added another dimension to my interests. Through IYRC's medicine programs, I've delved into research and community work. Throughout the program, I realized that while uncovering scientific mysteries is exhilarating, there's an undeniable magic in human connections.

So, what's the endgame? I want to be a bridge-builder, connecting cutting-edge tech with the beating heart of humanity. To transform the 'wow' of scientific breakthroughs into real-world 'how' that impacts lives. I believe the future isn't just about the coolest gadgets or the most mind-bending theories. But also about using them to understand each other better, solve meaningful problems, and perhaps sketch out a world that's a bit more awesome for everyone.
